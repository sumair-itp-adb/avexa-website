Waste Cooking Oil Recycling

Used cooking oil should never be disposed through drains or uncontrolled channels.

Responsible recycling helps protect the environment and supports long-term sustainability goals.

Biodiesel Support

Used cooking oil can contribute to biodiesel production and renewable energy initiatives.

AVEXA Recycling supports collection systems that strengthen the circular economy.

Knowledge Center

Can I Sell Used Cooking Oil?

Many restaurants and food businesses can responsibly dispose of used cooking oil through collection services.

What To Do With Leftover Cooking Oil?

Avoid dumping oil into drains. Store safely and provide it to responsible collection channels.

How Is Biodiesel Made From Used Cooking Oil?

Used cooking oil can be processed into biodiesel feedstock supporting renewable energy production.
